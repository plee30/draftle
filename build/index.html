How To Play

League of Legends team composition puzzle. Guess the set of 5 characters in their respective roles. All team compositions are from the 2022 LCS Regular Spring Season. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the answer.

Gray means that character is not in the answer.
Yellow means that character is answer, but in a different role.
Green means that character is answer and in that role.
